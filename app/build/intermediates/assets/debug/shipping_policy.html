How shipping cost is calculated?

While calculating shipping cost, it's just a work on "actual weight". We don't apply shipping on product quantity, but charges are applied on its weight. You will get the benefit of reduced shipping cost on each additional item, as the more will be the weight of the parcel, less charge will be applied per kg. We have fair & transparent shipping rates.

In short shipping rates are weight based. Weight of any product can be found on its detail page. To match the policies of the courier companies we use, all weights will be rounded up to the next 500 grams.
